About Munashe

Transforming complex ideas into compelling stories for modern African audiences.

Munashe Jumbe is a Zimbabwean entrepreneur, media personality, marketing strategist and youth development advocate dedicated to building impactful brands, businesses and communities across Africa.

With experience spanning media, digital marketing, brand communication, entrepreneurship, public speaking and youth empowerment, Munashe has worked with startups, corporates, NGOs, educational institutions and development organizations to create meaningful campaigns that drive engagement and real-world impact.

As the founder and leader behind multiple ventures focused on media, innovation, education and economic development, he specializes in shaping ideas into stories that resonate with Africa’s young people.
